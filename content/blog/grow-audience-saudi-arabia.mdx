# How to Grow Your X Audience in Saudi Arabia

Twitter (now X) remains one of the most influential social platforms in Saudi Arabia. With millions of active daily users, it's a primary channel for news, entertainment, and business.

If you're looking to build an audience in the Kingdom, generic advice won't cut it. You need a localized strategy.

## 1. Timing is Everything

Unlike Western markets where 9 AM - 5 PM are peak hours, the Saudi audience has a unique schedule:

- **Late Nights**: Engagement spikes between 9 PM and 1 AM.
- **Afternoon Lull**: 1 PM - 4 PM is generally quiet due to the work/school day and afternoon rest.
- **Ramadan Shift**: During the holy month, peak hours shift dramatically to 10 PM - 4 AM.

## 2. The Power of Threads (Sard - سرد)

"Sard" (threading) is incredibly popular in the Arabic content ecosystem. Users love deep dives into:

- Historical stories
- Business case studies
- True crime
- Personal development tips

Don't just tweet once. Tell a story across 10-15 tweets. Use hook images in the first tweet to grab attention.

## 3. Hashtags Matter More Here

In the US, hashtags are often seen as spammy. in KSA, they are the primary discovery engine.

- Monitor the "Trending in Saudi Arabia" list daily.
- Use relevant, high-volume tags (e.g., #السعودية, #RiyadhSeason).
- Avoid hashtag stuffing; 2-3 relevant tags are better than 10 random ones.

## 4. Visuals & Video

Text-only tweets perform okay, but video is king. Short, captioned clips (under 45 seconds) see 3x higher engagement rates.

If you can't produce video, use high-quality images or infographics. The Saudi audience appreciates polished aesthetics.

## 5. Engage Authentically

Reply to big accounts in your niche. Quote tweet with value. Don't just broadcast; build a community. The "Twitterati" in Saudi Arabia are a tight-knit group, and breaking in requires genuine interaction.
