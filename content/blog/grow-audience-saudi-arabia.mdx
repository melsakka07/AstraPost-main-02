# How to Grow Your X Audience in Saudi Arabia

Twitter (now X) remains one of the most influential social platforms in Saudi Arabia. With millions of active daily users, it's a primary channel for news, entertainment, and business.

If you're looking to build an audience in the Kingdom, generic advice won't cut it. You need a localized strategy that understands Saudi culture, timing, and content preferences.

---

## 1. Master the Saudi Timing Schedule

Unlike Western markets where 9 AM - 5 PM are peak hours, the Saudi audience has a unique schedule shaped by culture, climate, and work patterns.

### Peak Engagement Hours

| Time Period                | Engagement Level | Best Content Type               |
| -------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **9 PM - 1 AM**            | 🟢 Highest       | Conversations, threads, debates |
| **6 AM - 8 AM**            | 🟡 Medium        | Inspirational, news             |
| **1 PM - 4 PM**            | 🔴 Lowest        | Avoid posting                   |
| **10 PM - 4 AM** (Ramadan) | 🟢 Highest       | Spiritual, family content       |

### Why Late Nights Work

- Saudis are night owls by culture
- Cooler temperatures make evening hours ideal for social scrolling
- Post-work relaxation happens late at night
- Family gatherings often extend past midnight

## 2. Embrace the Power of "Sard" (سرد)

"Sard" — the Arabic term for threading — is incredibly popular in the Saudi content ecosystem. Users actively seek and share deep dives into topics they care about.

### High-Performing Thread Topics

| Topic Type                | Why It Works                       |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Historical Stories**    | Saudis love their rich history     |
| **Business Case Studies** | Entrepreneurship culture is strong |
| **True Crime**            | Massive engagement potential       |
| **Personal Development**  | Self-improvement is trending       |
| **Local Issues**          | Community discussions thrive       |

### Thread Structure Formula

```
Tweet 1: Hook + Eye-catching image
Tweet 2: Set the stage
Tweets 3-8: Main content with examples
Tweet 9: Key takeaway
Tweet 10: CTA (Follow for more)
```

**Remember:** Don't just tweet once. Tell a story across 10-15 tweets. Use hook images in the first tweet to grab attention in the timeline.

## 3. Hashtag Strategy for Saudi Arabia

In the US, hashtags are often seen as spammy. In KSA, they're the primary discovery engine.

### Essential Saudi Hashtags

| Hashtag         | Usage                 | When to Use      |
| --------------- | --------------------- | ---------------- |
| `#السعودية`     | General Saudi content | Almost always    |
| `#جدة`          | Jeddah-specific       | Local content    |
| `#الرياض`       | Riyadh-specific       | Local content    |
| `#RiyadhSeason` | Events/entertainment  | Seasonal content |
| `#ريادة_أعمال`  | Entrepreneurship      | Business content |

### Hashtag Best Practices

- ✅ Use 2-3 relevant hashtags per tweet
- ✅ Mix Arabic and English tags
- ✅ Monitor "Trending in Saudi Arabia" daily
- ❌ Don't stuff with 10+ random tags
- ❌ Avoid irrelevant trending tags

## 4. Visual Content That Works

Text-only tweets perform okay, but visuals dramatically increase engagement in the Saudi market.

### Content Performance Multipliers

| Content Type                | Engagement Boost |
| --------------------------- | ---------------- |
| **Short video (under 45s)** | `3x` higher      |
| **Infographics**            | `2.5x` higher    |
| **Quality photos**          | `2x` higher      |
| **Text only**               | Baseline         |

### What Saudis Love Visually

- **Coffee culture** — Traditional Saudi coffee cups
- **Landscapes** — Desert, cityscapes, modern architecture
- **Food** — Traditional dishes and presentations
- **Calligraphy** — Arabic typography and quotes
- **Events** — Riyadh Season, local festivals

**Pro Tip:** Use warm, golden tones in your visuals. They resonate better than cool, stark colors.

## 5. Engagement Strategies That Work

The Saudi Twitter community (often called "Twitterati") is tight-knit. Breaking in requires authentic engagement, not just broadcasting.

### Effective Engagement Tactics

1. **Reply to Big Accounts**
   - Add value to conversations
   - Share personal experiences
   - Ask thoughtful questions

2. **Quote Tweet with Value**
   - Add your perspective
   - Expand on the original point
   - Bring in local context

3. **Participate in Twitter Spaces**
   - Join conversations in your niche
   - Share expertise genuinely
   - Build relationships offline

4. **Support Other Creators**
   - Retweet valuable content
   - Add commentary when sharing
   - Celebrate others' wins

### What Doesn't Work

- ❌ Automated DMs
- ❌ Follow/unfollow tactics
- ❌ Generic "great post!" comments
- ❌ Self-promotion without value

## 7. Ramadan Special Strategy

During Ramadan, everything changes. Smart creators adapt their entire strategy.

### Ramadan Content Calendar

| Time           | Content Focus                          |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Suhoor**     | Spiritual motivation, quiet reflection |
| **Iftar**      | Family, food, gratitude                |
| **Late Night** | Community, entertainment, learning     |

### Ramadan-Specific Tips

- Share iftar experiences
- Post Quranic verses with reflection
- Discuss Ramadan traditions
- Support charity initiatives
- **Timing:** Shift all content to 10 PM - 4 AM
